Probiotics

Our clinically studied probiotic strain of Bacillus coagulans Unique-IS2® supports digestive health and stool regularity, and promotes a healthy microbiome that may help with nutrient availability for the gut cells and overall nutrient absorption. Gut health affects hormone metabolism and the gut-brain connection—the communication pathway between your digestive system and brain that influences mood, stress response, and energy levels. Since your gut produces about 90% of your body's serotonin (a key mood neurotransmitter), supporting gut health directly impacts how you feel.

Frequently Asked Questions

The formula was designed to contain ingredients that focus on and support areas like bone health, heart health, cellular longevity, and healthy aging, common health priorities in postmenopause. While there is nothing in the formulation that would interact directly with HRT, we always recommend discussing any new supplements with your qualified healthcare provider, including if you're on hormone therapy.

Our formula includes bone-supporting nutrients like vitamin D3, vitamin K2, calcium, and magnesium. However, for significant bone health concerns, we recommend working with your healthcare provider on a comprehensive approach that may include exercise, diet, and medical evaluation.

Chromium picolinate (Chromax®) in our formula helps support lean muscle mass and the body's metabolism, when combined with adequate protein and regular weight bearing exercise. Supporting lean muscle mass becomes especially important after menopause when declining estrogen makes it harder to maintain. Chromium is a mineral the body needs throughout life that works by helping your body unlock energy from food, supporting healthy blood sugar levels already in the normal range. When we age, and estrogen levels naturally decline, making sure we are getting appropriate levels of chromium can help support our metabolism by converting our nutrition into the fuel our muscles need, to stay lean.

Many women find that combining GLP-1 medications with targeted nutrition provides comprehensive support for their health goals. GLP-1s can sometimes affect muscle retention during weight loss, which is where our chromium picolinate and protein-supporting nutrients become especially valuable. However, we always recommend discussing any new supplements with your healthcare provider, especially when taking prescription medications, to ensure the best approach for your individual situation.
